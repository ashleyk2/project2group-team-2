Bleach Vol 1

It all starts here! How well does the original volume hold up after all these years? Check it out for yourself! I'd recommend checking out your local library for a copy first. Bleach is fairly popular, so it's possible they will have a copy. They might even have a digital copy you can download and read from your couch. If you know the website for your local library, just go there.

If you don't know your local library, to see if they have a copy of Vol 1, just click here to visit WorldCat and then put your zip code in the box that says 'Enter your location'. And a list of the closest libraries that have a copy should show up.


If your local library doesn't have a copy, the same Worldcat website has a link to several places you can purchase it from, including ABE Books, Amazona, and Barnes & Noble.
