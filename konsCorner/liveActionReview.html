Live Action Bleach Movie Review

Have you seen the live action adaptation of Bleach on Netflix yet? Wondering how it compares to the anime? Well look no further than our review to see if it's worth your time!
If you're hoping for a goofy action movie with giant swords and mythical creatures, you're in for a treat! The live action movie does simplify a few things, and longtime fans may be surprised if they expect to see a 100% faithful adaption. But overall, the film-makers found a good balance between staying faithful to the original, and telling a fun, action-packed story in the span of a typical movie. Rotten Tomatoes gave the movie an 80% fresh rating, and this reviewer agrees. So check it out on Netflix if you get a chance!
